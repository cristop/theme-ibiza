About

At Ibiza Property Collection, we help people buy and sell luxury real estate in one of the most sought-after locations and competitive markets in Europe.

Our experience of the Balearic property market, intimate knowledge of the islands and unique understanding of the lifestyle enables us to provide expert tailored advice and outstanding service, making us the first port-of-call for those looking to buy prestigious and luxury property in Ibiza.

With over 50 years of combined experience and as one of the most highly recommended real estate companies in the islands, our team has become the trusted, respected and connected face of the Balearic luxury real estate market. We deal with the finest on-market and off-market properties Ibiza has to offer, personally overseeing each transaction and ensuring that our clients view the best properties and receive premier service to gain complete competitive advantage.

Meet the team

Julian Cunningham-Bond

Julian’s reputation as the most trusted and respected real estate agent throughout the Balearic islands has been earned over many years. His career in real estate started over 20 years ago, in London, as an associate partner of the largest privately-owned estate agency in the world. He managed the Balearics and Caribbean desks, before moving to the Balearics over a decade ago and setting up his own real estate business. Julian is now considered the most influential real estate broker in the Balearics with an understanding of luxury real estate, valuation, negotiation, property law and the intricate layers of diplomacy and mediation that is unsurpassed.

Michelle Cunningham-Bond

Michelle has become one of the leading lights in the Balearic luxury property market over the past decade, managing her own successful property search agency in Mallorca - now the most successful within the Balearics. She has an extraordinary ability to combine her extensive knowledge of luxury properties in the Balearics with an appreciation of personalities, family dynamics and what people are looking for in a Balearic lifestyle. Quick to get to the core of an instruction, she is able to lay her finger on the ideal property with accuracy and alacrity. And with a referral rate of over 75%, her services are highly rated by those looking to find the perfect home in Mallorca and Ibiza.

Paul Charlton

Having lived with his family in Ibiza for over 20 years. During this time, he has gained a comprehensive knowledge of the island and a unique understanding of its character. Equipped with an enviable list of contacts and his ears and eyes permanently alert to the comings and goings in the Ibiza property market, Paul is an invaluable asset to those looking to buy or sell a second home, an investment property or a permanent home on the island.
