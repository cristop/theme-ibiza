Location

Blink and you'll miss it, San José (Sant Josep in Ibicenco) is the principal settlement in southern Ibiza.

This prosperous village's little plaza is overlooked by the blinding white facade of an 18th-century church and selection of engaging bar-cafes (some featuring live music) and a smattering of boutiques. San José is the perfect location for those prioritising connectivity and convenience, as it’s just a fifteen-minute drive from the airport and twenty minutes from Ibiza Town.

The village has a distinctly relaxed, genteel ambience and an international vibe, its surrounding hills topped with stunning modernist villas. Soaring over San José to the southwest is pine-clad Sa Talaia, the island's highest peak while a succession of drop-dead gorgeous cove beaches including Cala d'Hort and Cala Vedella are close by.
